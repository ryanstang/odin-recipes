Chocolate Chip Cookies

Home

Description

A chocolate chip cookie is a sweet baked treat that is recognized by its butter flavor and the inclusion of chocolate chips. Some variations can include nuts, oatmeal or raisins as well.

Cookie Dough

  1. Add butter in pan and let it melt slowly on medium heat. Once butter starts to bubble up, stir until you can see brown bits at bottom of pan.
  2. Transfer to mixing bowl and let it cool for 5-10 minutes.
  3. Add white and brown sugar to mixing bowl with brown butter. Mix until fully incorporated.
  4. Add one egg and mix again until texture is slimy.
  5. Add vanilla, salt and baking soda and mix again still fully incorporated.
  6. Add flour and chocolate chips in mix bowl. Fold and mix gently until there isn’t visually any white flour left.
  7. For more soft and chewy cookie, let cookie dough sit in fridge for 1-2 hours.

Baking

  1. Before baking, let dough sit in room temperature for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven at 350 degrees F.
  3. Using an ice cream scooper, scoop dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Keep about 2 inches apart from each cookie.
  4. Put cookies in the oven and bake for about 11 minutes.
  5. Rest on sheet for 5 minutes. Then transfer to a wire rack.
